| OP                        | Nor First (Top root)                | Nand first                         |
|---------------------------+-------------------------------------+------------------------------------+
| ~a                        | (nor a a)                           | (nand a a)                         |
| a & b                     | ~(nand a b)                         | ~(nor ~a ~b)                       |
| a or b                    | ~(nand ~a ~b)                       | ~(nor a b)                         |
| a xor b                   | ~(nand (~(nand ~a ~b)) (nor ~a ~b)) | ~(nor (~(nor a b)) (nand a b))     |
| a -> b  == (~a or b )     | ~(nand a ~b)                        | ~(nor ~a b)                        |
| a <-> b (Negato della xor | (nor (~(nor a b)) (nand a b))       | (nand (~(nand ~a ~b)) (nor ~a ~b)) |

a ^ b = ~(a & b) & (a | b)

// De morgan laws
~(F | G) == (~F & ~G)                    // legge di De Morgan
~(F & G) == (~F | ~G)                    // legge di De Morgan
